TOOLS

SP Flash Tool

SP Flash Tool is used for flashing firmware on Mediatek devices. It allows you to install custom ROMs and stock firmware.

Xiaomi Drivers

These drivers are necessary to connect your Xiaomi phone to your PC. They ensure proper communication for flashing or debugging.

Pixel Flasher

Pixel Flasher is a tool designed to flash Pixel ROMs. It allows users to flash official and custom firmware on their Pixel devices.

MTKClient

MTKClient is a powerful tool for Mediatek devices, offering features like unlocking bootloaders, flashing firmware, and debugging.

MiFlash Tool

MiFlash Tool is a tool to flash stock MIUI firmware on Xiaomi phones. It is simple and effective for restoring a bricked device.

XiaomiTool V2

This is a tool with which you will fix your bricked phone with a few simple clicks. In addition, you can use it to modify your phone a bit and, among other things, upload a different firmware. It's very simple (a kid who doesn't know anything about it would figure it out), but I know from my experience that it can mess up dm-verity verification and it won't pass properly.
